Trello cards thru email using Mailgun mailboxes.

  1. Setup your email and lists. You need to set the email yourself since Trello doesn't give out that information.

  2. Send an email to trello@marksteve.mailgun.org with a subject that contains the list keyword. CC those who you want to be added to the card. They should have setup their emails in the app as well.
